Welcome to Our Student Design Gallery

Explore the incredible design work created by our talented students. This gallery showcases a wide range of projects, including laser cutting, 3D printing, and other creative endeavors. About Us

We are a group of MYP students at Harvest International School, passionate about design and innovation. Our mission is to explore the world of design, showcase our work, and inspire others with our creative ideas. Through hands-on projects and collaborative efforts, we aim to push the boundaries of traditional design, learning from each other and experimenting with new technologies and techniques. Our work reflects our dedication to creativity, problem-solving, and the power of design to make a positive impact in the world.
